Prayer of Our CHURCH

Heavenly Father, in every age you have been our refuge. Yet again and still, we stand before you asking for your protection on your holy CHURCH For the victims of abuse and their families, pour out your healing and peace. For the bishops of this country, continue to inspire their decisions, and guide them with your Spirit.
